Root · Faction Picker

per the Law of Root, October 2025

Players 4

Everyone picks freely. The Law asks the chosen factions’ reach to add up to the recommended total for your player count (Law 5.2). Any mix of 17+ is allowed for adventurous groups.

Players & Seats (A.4)

Names are optional. Seating order and first player are randomized when you deal.

Draft Pool (A.8.1)

Tap to omit factions you don’t own or don’t want. The Vagabond and the Knaves of the Deepwood cannot both be included.
